Donating to Book Buddies

Interested in donating to Book Buddies? Our programs provide children with the drive to keep reading and eventually become well-read adults.

To make a monetary donation, contact Lawrence Langosta at lawrencelangosta@bookbuddies.com or call 217-867-5309.

For information about donating to upcoming book drives, contact Howard Homard at howardhomard@bookbuddies.com.

We look forward to working with you to provide a bright future for the children of Macon County.
