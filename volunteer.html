Become a Book Buddy!

Are you looking to volunteer in your community? Book Buddies is the perfect place to start. We offer a fun way to get involved with children in Macon County by reading with and playing educational games with local kids or working a book drive.

For information about volunteering, contact Lucy Loberts at lucyloberts@bookbuddies.com or call 217-555-5555.

We look forward to working with you to provide a bright future for the children of Macon County.
